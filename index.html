Pomegranate Cheesecake

This luscious pomegranate cheesecake combines creamy layers with a vibrant fruit glaze and crunchy seeds. Whether it's baked, no-bake, or mini versions, this dessert is perfect for holidays, special gatherings, or any time you crave something both beautiful and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 200g graham crackers or digestive biscuits, crushed
  • 75g melted butter
  • 500g cream cheese (softened)
  • 150g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup pomegranate juice
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • Pomegranate seeds for topping

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F). Line or grease a springform pan.
  2. Mix crushed crackers and melted butter. Press into the base. Bake for 10 minutes and cool.
  3. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, then mix in sour cream and vanilla.
  4. Pour over the crust and bake for 50–60 minutes until set but slightly jiggly.
  5. Cool at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Simmer pomegranate juice and cornstarch until thickened. Let cool, pour over cheesecake.
  7. Top with fresh pomegranate seeds before serving.

Tips & Nutritional Facts

  • Use full-fat cream cheese for best texture.
  • Don’t overmix the batter to avoid cracking.
  • Let it chill completely for a cleaner cut.
  • Calories: ~340 kcal per slice (1/12 of a 9-inch cake)
  • Protein: 6g | Fat: 24g | Carbs: 26g
  • Rich in: Vitamin C (from pomegranate), calcium
